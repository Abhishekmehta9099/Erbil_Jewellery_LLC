Our Story

With over 12 years of industry expertise, Erbil Jewellery LLC has established itself as a globally trusted name in the jewellery world. Since beginning operations in 2013, we have earned a reputation for delivering jewellery of exceptional quality, combining global vision with traditional craftsmanship and an unwavering commitment to detail and value. Our collections are meticulously designed and crafted by experienced professionals who bring passion and precision to every piece. From intricate detailing to consistent quality, Erbil Jewellery stands as a hallmark of trust and reliability for our customers, partners, and stakeholders. Our strong presence spans across 30+ countries and 6 continents, serving a distinguished global clientele. This global reach is a testament to our consistent adherence to stringent quality controls and timely delivery schedules. At Erbil Jewellery, customer satisfaction is our highest priority. Our management team is dedicated to providing a seamless and ethical experience—built on a foundation of quality, integrity, and excellence.

Our Mission

We aim to meet the diverse needs of our local and regional customers in gold jewellery and diamonds to help fuel their growth and to establish lasting relationships. Our mission is to remain true to our core principles of uncompromising integrity, outstanding service, and exceptional value.
